Vindinium

The Game

Vindinium is a multi-player game in which the goal of the game is to have to most gold at the end of the game. The game consists of 1200 turns. The way to obtain gold in through mines. The mines are guarded by goblins which the player has to attack the goblin in order to claim the mind, thus, losing health. However a mine is claimed, the player gets one gold each turn from each of the mines they have claimed.
Another way to earn gold is if one kills an opposing player. If the opposing player dies, they will respawn but all their mines will belong to the killer. But a player can heal before they die at a tavern. However, the tavern costs 2 gold to use. The only way the bots can be controlled is through coding their commands.
